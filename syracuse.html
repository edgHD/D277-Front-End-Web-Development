Syracuse

Syracuse is a vibrant city nestled in Central New York, known for its robust academic environment and rich industrial history. With a thriving community and diverse cultural offerings, Syracuse is a city that seamlessly blends tradition with modernity.

  • Population: 145,500 approx.
  • Year Incorporated: 1847
  • Region: Central New York
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income Level: $63,012 per year
Discover the dynamic culinary scene, immerse yourself in the local arts, and experience the warmth of its welcoming community. Syracuse is a city that captivates with its unique charm and spirited atmosphere.
