Buffalo

Buffalo, located on the shores of Lake Erie, is celebrated for its stunning architecture and diverse culture. As a city with a rich history and a thriving sports culture, Buffalo offers a unique blend of tradition and excitement.

  • Population: 273,720 approx.
  • Year Incorporated: 1832
  • Region: Western New York
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income Level: $66,571 per year
Savor the diverse culinary delights, explore the vibrant arts scene, and feel the energy of its passionate sports fans. Buffalo is a city that enchants with its distinctive character and lively spirit.
